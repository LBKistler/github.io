LB Kistler has worked with design and photography for both work and as a hobby for almost 30 years.


In 2019 LB became the Social Media Director for the William B. Harlan Memorial Library in Tompkinsville, Kentucky. In addition to creating a new logo for the library, she completely redesigned their website from the ground up. She revamped the existing Facebook accounts and expanded the library's social media platforms to include Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube. Her stylish posters, newspaper ads, and creative flair have made the library's social media reach one of the most lauded in Kentucky's Small and Rural Library community.
